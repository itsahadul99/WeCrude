Chicken meaty bone

Chicken heart

Chicken heart is a natural source of taurine, an essential amino acid for heart and eye health in dogs. It also contains iron, zinc and B vitamins, which support muscle development, the immune system and vitality.

Spinach

Spinach is a plant-based superfood rich in iron, antioxidants and fibre. It helps improve digestion, strengthens the immune system and provides vitamins A, C and K, essential for your pet's eye, skin and bone health.
